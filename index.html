Imposter Word Game

This is a social party game. Everyone takes turns checking their identity. One or more players are imposters, while others see a secret word. Find the imposter through discussion!

Game Rules

Imposter Word Game is a classic social deduction game for parties:

  • Identity Assignment: The system randomly assigns "Civilians" and "Imposters".
  • Secret Word: Civilians see a secret word, while the Imposter sees "You are the Imposter".
  • Description Round: Each player takes turns describing their word with one sentence. Don't be too obvious, or the imposter might guess the word!
  • Voting Round: After discussion, everyone votes for the most suspicious person.
